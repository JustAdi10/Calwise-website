Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Calwise handles your information when you use our iOS app and this website.

1. Information We Collect

Calwise is designed to store your nutrition and meal log data locally on your iPhone. We do not operate a backend service for meal history and we do not transmit your food log entries to our servers.

2. Health Data (HealthKit)

If you grant permission, Calwise may read from and/or write nutrition-related information using Apple HealthKit. HealthKit data is used only to provide app features you request. We do not sell HealthKit data and do not use it for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Calwise may use limited third-party services for app distribution and basic operational analytics, such as Apple App Store services. These services may process technical data under their own privacy terms. Calwise does not share your meal logs or personal nutrition history with third-party providers.

4. Data Retention

Because your app data is stored locally, retention is controlled by your device and usage choices. If you delete the app, local data may be deleted as part of iOS app removal behavior unless you have backups enabled through Apple services.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can edit or delete your logged entries directly in the app.
  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions anytime in iOS Settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Calwise is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product or legal changes. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact us at calwiseapp@gmail.com.
