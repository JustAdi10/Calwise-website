# Privacy Policy for Calwise

**Effective Date:** April 10, 2026  
**Last Updated:** May 8, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Calwise ("we," "us," "our," or the "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and related services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By downloading, installing, or using the Calwise app, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**
- Email address
- Password and authentication credentials
- Name and profile information
- Age, gender, height, and weight
- Fitness level and health goals

**Health & Fitness Data:**
- Dietary intake and food logs
- Calorie consumption and macronutrient data
- Weight tracking information
- Fitness goals and preferences
- Workout activities and exercise logs

**Communications:**
- Messages, feedback, and support requests you send to us
- Survey responses and feedback

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Apple Health Integration:**
With your explicit permission, we collect:
- Step count and daily activity data
- Workout activities and duration
- Weight and body measurements
- Nutritional data you choose to sync

**Usage Data:**
- App interaction patterns
- Features you access
- Time spent on different sections
- Device information (OS version, device model)
- Crash logs and error reports
- IP address and general location (city/country level)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Service Delivery:** Creating and managing your account, providing nutrition and fitness tracking features
- **Personalization:** Customizing your experience based on health goals and preferences
- **Analytics:** Understanding how you use the App to improve features and user experience
- **Communication:** Sending updates, responding to inquiries, and sending service notifications
- **Health Insights:** Providing nutritional analysis, calorie calculations, and progress tracking
- **Security:** Detecting fraud, preventing abuse, and protecting against unauthorized access
- **Legal Compliance:** Complying with applicable laws and regulations

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on **Supabase**, a cloud-based platform that provides:
- End-to-end encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
- Regular automated backups
- Database encryption at rest
- Compliance with international security standards

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement multiple layers of security:
- Secure authentication protocols
- Encrypted passwords and sensitive data
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls limiting data to authorized personnel only
- Secure API connections for all data transfers

**Important:** While we use industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, though we work diligently to protect your information.

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

### 5.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share your information only with:

**Supabase:**
- Backend infrastructure and database hosting
- Authentication and user management
- See their privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

**Apple Health (iOS):**
- Only with your explicit permission through iOS privacy controls
- Used for reading health metrics (steps, workouts, weight) and writing nutritional data
- Apple controls this data; we only access what you authorize
- See Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

### 5.2 Data We Do NOT Share

We **never**:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share data with marketers or advertisers without consent
- Disclose health data to unaffiliated companies
- Use your data for purposes other than those stated in this policy

### 5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when:
- Required by law or court order
- Necessary to protect our legal rights or prevent fraud
- You have given us consent to do so

## 6. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request access to all personal data we hold about you
- **Correction:** Request corrections to inaccurate data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Export:** Receive your data in a portable format

### 6.2 Apple Health Integration

You can at any time:
- Revoke Calwise's access to Apple Health through iOS Settings
- Manage which health metrics Calwise can read or write
- Clear synced data from Apple Health

### 6.3 Communication Preferences

You can opt out of:
- Non-essential promotional communications
- Marketing emails
- Push notifications (through app settings)

## 7. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained for the duration of your account. Upon deletion, data is securely removed within 30 days.
- **Health Data:** Retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual logs at any time.
- **Backup Data:** May be retained for up to 90 days for recovery purposes.
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated data retained for historical analysis (personally identifiable information removed).

## 8. Children's Privacy

Calwise is **not intended for children under 13 years of age**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete such data and terminate the child's account.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. By using Calwise, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.

## 10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of data sales
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at [privacy@calwise.app](mailto:privacy@calwise.app).

## 11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):
- Right of access to your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Rights related to automated decision-making

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer.
